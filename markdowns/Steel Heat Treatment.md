# Aircraft Heat Treatment Cheat Sheet

---

# Common Heat Treatment Processes

| Process | Purpose | Result | Memory Hook |
|----------|---------|--------|-------------|
| **Annealing** | Soften the metal | ↓ Hardness, ↑ Ductility | **Anneal = Soft** |
| **Normalizing** | Refine grain structure | Uniform strength & toughness | **Normalize = Uniform** |
| **Hardening** | Increase hardness | ↑ Hardness, ↑ Strength | **Hard = Strong** |
| **Tempering** | Reduce brittleness after hardening | ↑ Toughness, ↓ Brittleness | **Temper = Tough** |
| **Case Hardening** | Harden only the surface | Hard surface, tough core | **Hard Outside, Tough Inside** |
| **Stress Relieving** | Remove residual stresses | Improves dimensional stability | **Stress Out** |
| **Solution Heat Treatment** | Dissolve alloying elements | Prepares alloy for aging | **Solution First** |
| **Aging (Natural)** | Increase strength at room temperature | Moderate strengthening | **Nature Ages Slowly** |
| **Aging (Artificial)** | Increase strength by heating | Faster strengthening | **Artificial = Oven** |

---

# Heat Treatment Effects

| Process | Hardness | Strength | Ductility | Toughness |
|----------|:--------:|:--------:|:---------:|:---------:|
| Annealing | ↓ | ↓ | ↑ | ↑ |
| Normalizing | ↑ | ↑ | → | ↑ |
| Hardening | ↑↑ | ↑↑ | ↓ | ↓ |
| Tempering | ↓ (slightly) | ↓ (slightly) | ↑ | ↑↑ |
| Case Hardening | Surface ↑ | Surface ↑ | Core remains ductile | Core remains tough |

# Aluminum Heat Treatment Sequence

### Heat Treatable Alloys

```
Solution Heat Treatment
        ↓
      Quench
        ↓
      Aging
```

Memory Hook:

> **Heat → Quench → Age**

---

### Aging Types

| Type | Temperature | Memory Hook |
|------|-------------|-------------|

| Artificial Aging | Elevated Temperature | **Artificial = Oven** |

# Quenching Media

| Medium | Cooling Rate | Typical Use |
|---------|--------------|-------------|
| Brine | Fastest | Maximum hardness |
| Water | Fast | Carbon steels |
| Oil | Moderate | Alloy steels |
| Air | Slow | Air-hardening steels |

Memory Hook:

> **Brine → Water → Oil → Air**

(Fastest → Slowest)

- **Anneal** → Soft
- **Normalize** → Uniform
- **Harden** → Strong
- **Temper** → Tough
- **Case Harden** → Hard Outside
